## HDMI (High-Definition Multimedia Interface)
![499](<./assets/3.2 Video Cables/file-20260306185524058.png>)[^2]
- **Video and audio stream**
	- All digital, no analog
	- ~20 meter maximum distance before losing too much signal
- **19-pin (Type A) connector**
	- One of the most common video connector types

## DisplayPort
![568](<./assets/3.2 Video Cables/file-20260306190205648.png>)[^3]
- **Digital information sent in packetized form**
	- Like Ethernet and PCI Express
	- Carries both audio and video
- **Compatible with HDMI and DVI**
	- Passive adapter
	- DisplayPort -> HDMI
	- DisplayPort -> DVI
- **A full-size interface may include a locking mechanism**
	- Don't pull without unlocking

## DVI (Digital Visual Interface)
![471](<./assets/3.2 Video Cables/file-20260306191406462.png>)[^4]
- **Single and dual link video**
	- Single link; 3.7 Gbps (1920 x 1200 at 60 Hz)
	- Dual link; 7.4 Gbps (2560 x 1600 at 85 Hz)
	- No audio support
- **DVI-A**
	- Analog signals
	- Backward compatibility with VGA
- **DVI-D**
	- Digital signals
- **DVI-I**
	- Integrated - Digital and analog in the same connector
![689](<./assets/3.2 Video Cables/file-20260306191703294.png>)[^5]
- One of the challenges of DVI is that it's a single connector with multiple types of connections.
- DVI only sends video support.

## VGA (Video Graphics Array)
![432](<./assets/3.2 Video Cables/file-20260306212210123.png>)[^6]
- **DB-15 connector**
	- More accurately called *DE-15*
	- 15 pins
	- The connector itself is shaped like the letter "D".
- **Blue color**
	- PC System Design Guide
- **Video only**
	- No audio signal
- **Analog signal**
	- No digital
	- The image degrades after 5 to 10 meters


## [USB-C](<./3.2 Peripheral Cables.md>)
![614](<./assets/3.2 Video Cables/file-20260306212444518.png>)[^7]
- **A connector**
	- Not a protocol
	- Can send many different signals over that particular connector
- **Many uses for USB-C:**
	- Power
	- USB data
	- Thunderbolt data
	- DisplayPort video
	- HDMI video (not common)
	- Mobile High-Definition Link (MHL)
