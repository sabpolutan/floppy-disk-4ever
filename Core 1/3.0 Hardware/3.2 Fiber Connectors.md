# Fiber Connectors - CompTIA A+ 220-1201 - 3.2
Source: [Fiber Connectors - CompTIA A+ 220-1201 - 3.2](https://youtu.be/Qxbp-c23cYY?si=PlD0owH_x4n9vQiT)

When working with fiber optics, you may find some standard interfaces.

![](<./assets/3.2 Fiber Connectors/file-20260306165153780.png>)[^1]

## ST- Straight Tip
- **Bayonet connector**
	- Stick and twist
- **Push on and turn**
	- Locks in place
		- You push it in and slightly twist it to lock it in place.
	- Turn to unlock
		- To remove it, you need to untwist it, then remove it from the connector.

## SC - Subscriber Connector
- **Not actually an abbreviation**
	- We've created our own names
	- Also known as:
		- Square connector
		- Standard connector
- **Pushes on to lock**
	- Pull the connector to unlock
- **A popular fiber connector**
	- Common in many data centers 

## LC - Lucent Connector
- **Another popular fiber type**
	- Smaller and more compact connector
- **Other names**
	- Local connector
	- Little connector
